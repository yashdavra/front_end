Moons of the Solar System

Moon (Luna)

Earth's only natural satellite.
Diameter: 3,474.8 kilometers (2,159.2 miles).
Distance from Earth: Approximately 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles).
It is the fifth-largest moon in the Solar System.

Phobos

One of the two moons of Mars.
Diameter: Approximately 22.2 kilometers (13.8 miles).
It orbits very close to Mars and is one of the smallest moons in the Solar System.

Deimos

Mars' other moon.
Diameter: Approximately 12.4 kilometers (7.7 miles).
It is the smaller and more distant of Mars' two moons.
